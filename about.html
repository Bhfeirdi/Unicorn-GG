Hello and welcome!
We are Notail, Ceb and Ana from Unicorn GG. Together we have put in place what we believe to be the greates LAN and competitive gaming experience in Ireland today.
We were all born with controllers in hand and with the rise of ESports over the last decade we've travelled the world together to every big stadium event we could.
After years as fans we decided to bring our passion for competitive LAN events back home and make it the best experince we possibly could. Bring your teams, bring your friends, we can't wait to see you here!
